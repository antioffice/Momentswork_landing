Beautiful workspaces on hourly and daily basis.
Catering to the increasingly mobile and on-the-go workforce, momentswork helps its clients—freelancers, entrepreneurs, and remote workers—to find, and access beautiful workspaces, while paying on hourly basis.
We are actively growing, and looking for partners to join our network.
Currently, we work with coworkings, and serviced offices, and soon will be offering smart locks that will allow to access individual spaces.
How it works
  1. You sign up, list your desk or space, and name the price per hour.
  2. Our clients discover and book your desk or space, while you receive booking notification through a friendly messenger interface on your smartphone.
  3. Upon arrival and departure, you check in and check out visitors with one click.
  4. We calculate the sum and transfer money to your account.
Monetize your free space
Tap into the network of active, increasingly mobile users—remote workers, freelancers, entrepreneurs—to maximize your property revenue.
Hassle free
We take care of bookings, accounting, checkins, and checkouts. You provide the space and get the money.
On your terms
You are in full control of quantity of spaces you list, and booking durations.
